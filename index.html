EACHE Period

A simple and elegant app for viewing EACHE Period's information.

How to Download & Use

iOS:

Unfortunately you are not able to install the app on your phone but you can test the app on your phone. Alternatively you can test it in a browser on your phone using the web instructions.

  1. First you must be on the "stuff" WiFi.
  2. Next install Expo Go.
  3. Then open the Expo Go app.
  4. Click on the option to open the QR code scanner.
  5. Scan this QR Code.

Web:

If you do not have your phone you are able to test EACHE Period in your browser.

  1. First you must be on the "stuff" WiFi.
  2. Open your web browser.
  3. Go to this URL.

Android:

  1. Go to the app Releases.
  2. Download the apk file of the latest version.
  3. After downloading, open the apk file.
  4. There should be a popup asking you for permission to install the app. Click accept and it should install successfully.
  5. Launch the app and enjoy!
